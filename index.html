Biography

I am currently a Ph.D. student in Computer Science and Technology at Zhejiang University, fortunately advised by Prof. Chunhua Shen and Prof. Hao Chen. I transferred from the master's program to the Ph.D. program in 2025.

My research focuses on generative models, autoregressive video generation, world models, and customized generation and editing. Before joining Zhejiang University, I received my B.Eng. degree in Software Engineering from the University of Electronic Science and Technology of China.
